About

My pathway until now

  • 1999-2002

    The beginning

    Ranked 4th out 1200 candidates to the position of Informatics Technician at MPU, government institution, I was very excited! Tech Support was my name! It doesn't matter the device! CPU formatting, HD images creation, antivirus installation, printers fixing, backup Creation and restoring... There was no bad time! Everything could be solved at IT world!

  • 2002-2008

    The bar raises, but so does the opportunities!

    Still at MPDFT, recently admitted by the systems development team, a broad universe of systems and programming languages was learnt, going from Delphi, until Java, passing through website development using ASP. Responsible tom maintain until 30 systems simultaneously.

  • 2009-nowadays

    Alternative ways to emprisonment and eficiency improvements in their application

    In the Department of Alternative Ways of Emprisonment, strategic inside MPDFT and responsible for monitoring authors of minor crimes, was responsible for develop a system capable to register and control the compliance of tasks by convicted ones, like payments to NGOs or mandatory services done for those institutions. After implantation and continuous system evolution, the control done by the department was not only improved, but the values paid to the institutions has been increasing in an order of 12% yearly, helping those NGOs to better aid the society.

  • 2016-present

    A new adventure

    After the conclusion of a specialization as a Full Stack Developer, at the University of Hong Kong/Coursera, I have done some freelance work, using mainly the MEAN Stack (MongoDB, Express, AngularJS, NodeJS), always trying to use the most recent technologies for both Front-end and Back-end. Besides that, I am always studying the best practices of programming, like Mobile First design and Agile Development Techniques.
